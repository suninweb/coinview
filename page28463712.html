What is Permanent Makeup?
Permanent makeup, also called a cosmetic tattoo, is done with a pen that tattoos the skin in areas where makeup is usually applied.

The effect lasts for several years, after which you can again book an appointment with your beautician.

Permanent makeup differs from ordinary tattoos by the pigments used: they are more gentle and don’t stay under the skin forever, making the treatment safer.
